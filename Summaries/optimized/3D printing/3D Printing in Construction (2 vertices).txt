The application of 3D printing technology has expanded across various industries, including the construction sector. This technology enables the creation of 3D printed concrete structures. The integration of 3D printing in construction covers material requirements and commonly used building materials, and also highlights the potential challenges and future prospects of this technology. A 3D concrete printer capable of printing ready-mixed concrete has been developed, a significant advancement in the field. The 3D printing process for a power distribution substation involves equipment, software, material preparation, printing procedure, and construction inspection. The current limitations of 3D concrete printing are emphasized, while also presenting innovative solutions and practical applications. Addressing these challenges and sharing construction experiences contributes to the advancement of 3D printing technology in the construction industry, offering valuable insights for future developments.
