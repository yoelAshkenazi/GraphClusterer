Composite materials and their applications cover a range of topics, including the development and applications of green composite materials, which are sustainable and environmentally friendly, with a focus on green metal-matrix composites, highlighting their chemical composition, properties, and fabrication techniques. Dynamic materials, including vibrational dynamics, explore how certain materials can change their properties under vibration. Viscoelastic composite materials have applications in construction, while graphene and its derivatives as nanofillers in polymers enhance their properties for various industrial uses. Analytical models evaluate residual stresses in polymeric composite materials, emphasizing the importance of accurate resin modeling. Composite materials are used in railway sleepers to increase load capacity and improve the railroad system for heavier and faster trains, showcasing diverse applications and benefits, from environmental sustainability to improved mechanical properties, and the importance of understanding their behavior through various analytical approaches.
