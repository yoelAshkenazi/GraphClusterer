Autonomous technology and its applications are being explored, specifically focusing on the Internet of Autonomous Things (IoAT) and its impact on different industries. The IoAT plays a significant role in healthcare, military, transportation, aerospace, and home robotics, with transportation being the most prominent application. A flexible printed IoT antenna has been developed for drone-based sensors, showcasing the integration of IoT and autonomous drones. Flying ad hoc networks (FANETs) and the use of reinforcement learning for efficient routing protocols are also being explored, addressing challenges like node mobility and topology changes. This aims to improve network communication between drones, emphasizing the importance of low delay and energy efficiency. Overall, there is a growing interest and advancements in autonomous technologies, IoT integration, and their potential to revolutionize various sectors.
