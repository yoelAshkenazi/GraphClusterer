Mesa Royalty Trust has announced a distribution to its unit holders, totaling $55,192, which equates to 2.96 cents per unit, to be paid on April 30th to those registered as unit holders on March 31st. BRNF Liquidating Trust has declared a 13th liquidating dividend for its Series A, B, and C certificates, amounting to $1.10 per unit, scheduled to be paid on April 1st to holders of record as of March 18th. The trust previously issued a 12th liquidating distribution in January, with varying amounts for each series of certificates. These announcements highlight the ongoing financial activities of both trusts, ensuring that unit holders and certificate holders receive their respective distributions.
