The advancements in small aerial drone technology have given rise to various innovative applications, and the BeeCluster system has been developed to support this progress. BeeCluster is a drone management platform that aims to streamline the use of drone fleets for various tasks, with a primary focus on predictive optimization, predicting future application tasks and creating optimized flight plans for drones, ensuring efficient geographical sensing operations. By providing a virtual drone abstraction, BeeCluster allows developers to easily define sensing tasks and automatically assigns these tasks to the fleet, minimizing the overall execution time, and is designed to enhance the effectiveness of drone-based applications in areas such as mapping, environmental monitoring, and emergency response.
