Various applications and advancements in open-source technology are being made, particularly in the field of autonomous vehicles. Open-Source Tools and Open-Source Software are growing in popularity in Unmanned Aerial Vehicles, enhancing UAV technology through autopilot firmware, ground station software, and photogrammetry data processing. A low-cost, open-source thruster for autonomous underwater or surface vehicles has been designed using 3D printing and open-source software, offering a solution for researchers and students in developing countries. A portable drone design for educational purposes emphasizes affordability and component combinability, featuring a Y6 frame module, water protection, and propeller optimization. Additionally, a Multiset Rewriting language has been proposed for specifying and analyzing Time-Sensitive Distributed Systems, particularly in the context of autonomous drones, introducing concepts such as realizability and survivability.
