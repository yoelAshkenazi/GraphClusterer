Autonomous technology and its applications are discussed with a focus on the Internet of Autonomous Things (IoAT) and its potential across different sectors. The distribution of IoAT applications across industries highlights transportation, military, aerospace, healthcare, and home robotics as key areas, with deep learning and AI as foundational technologies. A flexible IoT antenna for drone-based applications has been developed, with details on materials and manufacturing process. Flying ad hoc networks (FANETs) and reinforcement learning (RL) are used to optimize routing protocols for efficient communication between drones, introducing the concept of RL, the Markov decision process, and discussing challenges and opportunities. The diverse applications and ongoing research in autonomous technology emphasize the role of AI and learning algorithms in enhancing performance and efficiency across different sectors.
