Green composite materials are environmentally friendly and made from recyclable resources, with growing importance in various industries due to their eco-friendly nature and desirable physical and mechanical properties. Green metal-matrix composites, dynamic materials such as vibrational dynamics and kinetic dynamic materials, and viscoelastic composite materials are specific types of composites with unique properties and applications. Graphene and its derivatives have unique properties and applications in enhancing the performance of polymers and other industrial materials. Analytical models are used to evaluate residual stresses in polymeric composite materials, and composite materials are used in railway sleepers to increase their load-bearing capacity. These examples showcase the diverse applications and benefits of composite materials, from their environmental advantages to their ability to enhance the properties of various industrial materials and infrastructure components.
