Germany, officially the Federal Republic of Germany, is a fascinating European country with a rich history and diverse culture. It is a federal state comprising sixteen states, including city-states like Berlin and area states like Bavaria and Saxony. The city of Heidelberg, known for its castle ruins and the iconic Old Bridge, is a notable tourist destination in the state of Baden-Württemberg. With a population of over 82 million, Germany is the most populous member state of the European Union, and its cities, such as Erfurt and Jena in Thuringia, play significant roles in the country's political and cultural landscape. The name Jens, popular in Denmark, has German origins, reflecting the interconnectedness of European cultures. Germany's architecture, from the Church of the Holy Spirit in Heidelberg to the federal parliament building in Berlin, showcases a range of styles, attracting millions of visitors each year.
