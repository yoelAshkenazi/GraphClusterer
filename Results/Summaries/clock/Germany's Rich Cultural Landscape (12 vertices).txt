Germany, a federal state with sixteen constituent states, showcases a rich history and diverse cultural landscape. Cities like Heidelberg, with its iconic castle and Old Bridge, and Erfurt, the capital of Thuringia, attract visitors with their historical charm. The country's architecture, such as the Church of the Holy Spirit in Heidelberg, reflects Romanesque and Gothic styles. The sixteen states, including city-states like Berlin and area states like Bavaria, contribute to Germany's federal structure. With a population of over 82 million, Germany is the most populous member state of the European Union, offering a blend of traditional and modern attractions, from historic landmarks to vibrant student cities.
