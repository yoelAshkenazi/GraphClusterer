A range of medical topics are discussed, including migraine headaches, the selection of a pediatrician, the use of Imitrex for migraine treatment, the difference between Medical Doctors (M.D.) and Doctors of Osteopathy (D.O.), the treatment of sinus and allergy symptoms, the diagnosis and treatment of Crohn's disease, the potential benefits and risks of alternative treatments, the impact of diet on health, the diagnosis and treatment of epilepsy, the use of acupuncture, the role of B12 supplements in vegan diets, the treatment of arrhythmia, the management of chigger bites, and the diagnosis and treatment of colon cancer. Personal experiences, opinions, and anecdotes related to these medical topics are also included.
