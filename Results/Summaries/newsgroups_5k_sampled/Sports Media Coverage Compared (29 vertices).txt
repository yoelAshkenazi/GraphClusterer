Sports media coverage is discussed, focusing on radio stations and television networks. Participants share opinions on sports radio stations like WFAN in New York, WIP in Philadelphia, and KDKA in Pittsburgh, comparing strengths and weaknesses. They discuss sportswriting quality in different cities and newspapers, such as the Washington Post, Philadelphia Inquirer, and New York Times. The conversation extends to television coverage, specifically ESPN's programming decisions, including disappointment with prioritizing a baseball game over a hockey game's final moments. The NHL's return to American network TV and challenges with local preemptions and lack of promotion are mentioned. The group debates the best cities for sports coverage, comparing sportswriters and columnists' expertise and ability to attract high-profile guests for interviews.
