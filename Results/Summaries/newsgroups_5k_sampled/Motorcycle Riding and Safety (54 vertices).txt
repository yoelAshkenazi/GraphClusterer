Motorcycles involve various topics such as riding techniques, safety, and equipment. Countersteering is a main topic, with some riders arguing it is an essential skill to learn, while others believe it is unnecessary and confusing for new riders. Formal training and rider education are also discussed, with riders sharing their experiences with rider courses and the benefits of learning proper riding techniques. Helmet safety, riding in traffic, and countersteering in emergency situations are other topics. Radar detectors, regular helmet inspection, and the impact of a rider's emotional state on their riding speed are also mentioned.
