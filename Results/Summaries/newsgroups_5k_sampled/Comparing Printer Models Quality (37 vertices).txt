Several discussions and inquiries regard various printers, focusing on inkjet and laser printers. The Canon BJ-200 is a popular choice, praised for its fast drying speed, high resolution, and overall print quality. Some compare it to the HP DeskJet 500, with the BJ-200 being faster and having better resolution, although the DeskJet is known for its sturdy build and compatibility with Windows soft fonts. The HP LaserJet IIIP and 4M are also mentioned, with the 4M offering superior print quality and being a newer model. Users emphasize the importance of print quality, resolution, and compatibility with various software and operating systems, sharing experiences with specific models like the Okidata 180, Apple Laserwriter IIg, and Hewlett Packard toner cartridge, and discussing upgrades like adding PostScript support and increasing resolution.
