X Window System and related technologies, such as X terminals, window managers, and software development, are discussed in terms of various aspects. Topics covered include graphics software, Xt and Motif programming, image processing, benchmarking, and hardware compatibility. Specific software and hardware, such as Xterm, XtAppAddTimeOut, and NCD X-terminals, are also mentioned. Information and advice are provided on various issues related to X Window System and its usage.
