A broad range of topics is covered, including architecture and urban planning, international relations, history, and technology. The Neoclassical and Art Deco movements are discussed, with the Arc de Triomphe du Carrousel in Paris serving as an example of Neoclassical design. The history of aviation is touched upon, including the development of aerodromes and the earliest aircraft hijackings. The Syrian Civil War, the insurgency in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, and the fall of the Assad regime are mentioned, as well as the British South Africa Company's administration of Rhodesia. Various legal and political concepts are covered, such as the lack of a universally accepted definition of terrorism, the legal status of non-combatants, and the structure of the United Nations System. Geographical and cultural insights are provided, describing locations like New York Harbor, Canary Wharf in London, and Saint Isaac's Square in Saint Petersburg. The concept of society and its characteristics are discussed, along with the role of language, highlighting Filipino, Tagalog, and English in the Philippines. Technological advancements, such as smart TVs and the Ultra High Frequency (UHF) radio frequency range, are also mentioned.
