Various government agencies and divisions are responsible for transportation, law enforcement, and military operations. The Special Activities Center is a CIA division for covert and paramilitary operations, with two groups focused on tactical operations and political action. The New York City Subway serves multiple boroughs and is overseen by the New York City Department of Transportation. The Air Support Division of the Los Angeles Police Department is the largest municipal airborne law enforcement unit in the US, supporting various police activities. The Federal Highway Administration manages highway transportation programs. The Virginia Department of Transportation maintains and operates Virginia's transportation infrastructure. The 160th Special Operations Aviation Regiment provides helicopter aviation support for US Army special operations. These organizations play crucial roles in their respective fields, ensuring public safety, transportation efficiency, and military capabilities.
