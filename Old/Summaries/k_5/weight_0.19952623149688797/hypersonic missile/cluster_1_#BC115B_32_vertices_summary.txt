An Integrated Fuzzy Guidance (IFG) law for a surface to air homing missile is introduced.The introduced approach is a modification of the well-known Proportional Navigation Guidance (PNG) law. The IFG law enables the missile to approach a high maneuvering target while trying to minimize control effort as well as miss distance in a two-stage flight.To determine the nature and extent of Iranian casualties by ballistic missile attacks during the eight years of the Iraq-Iran war.The data collected about Iraqi missile strikes on Iranian cities included the following: date and time, number and type of missiles, cities targeted, and injuries and deaths resulting from impacts of missiles in civilian areas..
