In the Middle East region, desalination technology is gaining higher attention as compared to other part of the globe. In this article, we have carried out a study to evaluate the current status of small modular nuclear reactors and the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) assisted desalination projects for the Middle East and North Africa region, developing and developed countries of the world. The potential of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA) for adopting nuclear reactors and coupled nuclear reactor desalination is also discussed.The article presents an overview of the issues for ensuring safe operation of the Russian nuclear power plants with RBMK-1000 reactors..
